About Us

YUVG provides students passionate about venture capital and entrepreneurship with the opportunity to conduct research and analysis on real startups, similar to the responsibilities of venture capitalists. Unlike most venture capital groups, YUVG partners directly with VC firms in order to provide due-diligence and make investment recommendations for them – a unique experience for undergraduates interested in VC and technology).

In addition to taking on the role of analysts, another subset of members in our club will be working directly with startups in the Yale entrepreneurial ecosystem, including conducting market research on their behalf. We also plan to connect the startups that we partner with at Yale to our extensive network of VCs, especially during early-stage funding rounds.

We work alongside VCs.

YUVG partners with VCs in all industries and provide our services, including in-depth due-diligence and sourcing. Our team is composed of seasoned investors who are able to independently identify, build, and validate investment hypotheses in industries ranging from hard tech, to healthcare, to construction.

We support Yale startups.

Yale's entrepreneurial ecosystem is growing rapidly every year. However, existing institutions at Yale are not sufficient for connecting student-founded startups with investors. We are filling this gap at Yale by being the hub for investors at Yale. Our team works with our VC partners to help secure funding for Yale-based startups.
