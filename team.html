Meet the Team!

Alex Chun

Alex is a sophomore at Yale studying MechE + Econ. As a managing partner at Chalk, he writes first $100K checks for early-stage deep-tech startups. Aside from YUVG and Chalk, Alex also has experience interning at VC firms, starting his own fintech startup, and conducting research. Deeply passionate about robotics and aero/astro, he is a researcher at the Yale Social Robotics Lab and UNLV Drones and Autonomous Systems Lab as well as a FAA 107 Certified Drone Pilot!

Vanessa Li

Vanessa is a multifaceted student who has lived in China, Hong Kong, and the US with fluency in multiple languages. She is a startup founder and team player. She has interned at Scouts Venture to find and conduct due diligence on early stage SaaS ventures. She is also a scientific researcher in molecular biology and biochemistry with published research papers.

Andy Yang

Andy is a sophomore from Atlanta studying CS + Econ. Last summer, he researched private equity funds and co-investment opportunities at the Georgia Tech Foundation. This upcoming summer, he will be working at ICONIQ Capital as an investment strategy intern. Outside of YUVG, Andy loves cheering on Atlanta’s sports teams, beating his friends in poker, and playing soccer.

Asher Ellis

Asher is a sophomore from San Diego studying Applied Mathematics. He has had several experiences in venture capital, ranging from data operations at 500 Global to scouting at shl.vc. Outside of YUVG, Asher spends his time training in the Air Force ROTC program, hoping to pursue a defense career. Feel free to message him about coffee, policy, or hiking!
